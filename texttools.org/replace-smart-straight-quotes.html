About

Use this text tool to replace curly/smart quotes with regular straight quotes and vice versa.

Use Straight Quotes

This option aggressively converts all curly/smart quotes including a single opening (‘), single closing (’), double opening (“), double closing (”), single prime (′), double prime (″), and triple prime (‴) to regular straight quotes (" and ').

Use Smart Quotes

This option replaces straight quotes with curly quotes. It will diligently identify where to place curly quotes instead of simply replacing all (" and ') with (“ and ’). It can automatically distinguish the beginning and the end of the quote, conjunction's possession, abbreviated years, etc.
