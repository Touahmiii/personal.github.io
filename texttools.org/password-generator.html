About

Use this tool to generate strong, secure, and valid passwords. You can specify how many passwords you want to generate (default is 1) and the length of the password (default is 10 and minimum is 4). For maximum security, the generated password/s will always be completely random, and always include at least 1 lower-case letter a-z, 1 an upper-case letter A-Z, 1 digit 0-9, and 1 special character. The result will be a compliant password that meets the security standards on any website.

You can also directly print the generated passwords by clicking "File" and then "Print". To download a copy to your computer, choose "Save as".

What Makes a Password Strong?

Complexity is the key. A strong password must be at least 8 characters long and must include at least one letter (uppercase or lowercase), at least one number, and at least one special character from the following `[email protected]#$%^&*()_-+={}[]\|:;"'<>,.?/, and must not consist of repetitive characters.
