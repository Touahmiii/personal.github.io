About

Use this text tool to generate a filler or dummy text for your mockup design or web page template. You can choose how many paragraphs you want to generate at a time. Each generated paragraph is composed of a randomized set of sentences from Lorem Ipsum, the most common placeholder text used for demonstration purposes. Enable the “HTML Tag” option to wrap each paragraph with <p> tag.
